How to win: Make all the cells black.

How to play: Click on a cell to reverse its color and the colors of its neighbors.

Tip: Clicking twice the same cell cancels the action.

Corollary: You never need to click a cell twice. A level with n cells can ALWAYS be completed with n clicks or less.
